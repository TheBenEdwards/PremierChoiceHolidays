Premier Choice Holidays provides cruises to Most countries within the EU and America, with some of the Cruises going to South America. Safari

Safaries that Premier Choice Holidays provide are all based in South Africa. Accommodation is provided with the Safari. Safaris last up to a week but can be anything shorter than that. You can expect to go out on a "Game" up to 3 times a day, one in the morning, one midday and one in the evening. Prices vary depending on the duration of the stay. You can organise a Safari now Here

Train Journey

Premier Choice Holidays provides Train Journeys as a Holiday Option. Prices depend on how many Locations you plan to stop at. Accommodation is NOT provided by Premier Choice Holidays, and must be organised separately. It is advised to book your Train journey well in advance so that you can book your hotels with plenty of time. Train Journeys can be organised Here
